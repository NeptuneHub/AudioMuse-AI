From this page you can create both normal users and additional admin users. Normal users can sign in and use AudioMuse-AI but cannot see the Administration menu (Setup Wizard, Users, Cleaning, Scheduled Tasks, Backup, Provider Migration, Analysis and Clustering) or manage other users. Admin users have full access, can manage other users, and can delete each other - but nobody can delete their own account, and at least one admin must always exist. Passwords are stored hashed with argon2 and are never shown again after creation.
