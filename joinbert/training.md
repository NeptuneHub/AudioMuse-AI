# Training Data — Provenance & Attribution

## Summary

The JointBERT router training set (10,411 examples) comprises data from open-source and AI-augmented sources:

| Source | Examples | License |
|--------|----------|---------|
| Synthetic templates | 5,330 | AGPLv3 |
| SNIPS NLU Benchmark (PlayMusic) | 1,405 | Apache 2.0 |
| CPCD (Google Research) | 722 | CC-BY 4.0 |
| User curated v1 | 63 | AGPLv3 |
| Paraphrase augmentation (v1) | 891 | AGPLv3 |
| **AI-generated augmentation** | **2,063** | **AGPLv3** |

Validation data: 881 examples (mixed provenance for generalization measurement).


---

## Training Data Composition

**Synthetic templates** (5,330 examples, AGPLv3) — generated by [generate_data.py](generate_data.py) using template combinations with artist/song names (factual references, not copyrightable) and generic vocabulary (genres, moods, lyrics topics).

**Open-source human-written data** (2,190 examples):
- SNIPS NLU benchmark PlayMusic intent (1,405 examples, Apache-2.0)
- CPCD with Doh et al. intent annotations (722 examples, CC-BY 4.0)

**User-curated examples** (63 examples, AGPLv3) — real-world patterns not covered by synthetic or imported data.

**Paraphrase augmentation** (891 examples, AGPLv3) — rule-based variants (carrier-phrase substitution, synonym substitution, word-order variation, casing/punctuation) applied to ~500 sampled entries for diversity.

**AI-generated augmentation** (2,063 examples, AGPLv3) — sampled from training data only, AI-modified variants where semantically meaningful elements were altered (intent, slots, or phrasing) to expand coverage while maintaining label consistency.
